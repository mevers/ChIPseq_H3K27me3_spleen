# Raw sequencing data

Reference epigenome series in spleen (Mus musculus, adult, 8 week)
Targets: H3K27ac, H3K4me3, H3K36me3, H3K27me3, H3K4me1, Control
Lab: ENCODE Consortium (Bing Ren, UCSD)
Project: ENCODE
Link: https://www.encodeproject.org/reference-epigenomes/ENCSR902FHL/


# Sequencing data specifications

Paired-end or single-end: Single-end
Read length: 36 bp
Read depth: 30 - 45 million reads
